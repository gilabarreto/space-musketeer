Space Musketeer


Space Musketeers is a space game where you play as Elon the Musketeer, a space ranger trying to colonize Mars. The planet is inhabited by hostile aliens, Tweeters, who oppose colonization. You must use your spacecraft, the EagleY, to defeat them and ensure the safety of colonists.

Victor Barreto, a Full Stack Web Developer and retro game enthusiast, re-created and developed the game. Click here to learn more about the author and his other projects.
